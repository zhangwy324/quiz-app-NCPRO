Directions:

NCPRO staff would be divided into four groups based on your date of birth. Staff who were born in first quarter would be in group 1, born in second quarter would be in group 2, etc.

Two volunteers from each group will participate in this multiple-choice question social game. The volunteer must answer questions correctly to stay in the game, he/she is out if the question was answered incorrectly.

All staff in his/her assigned group can help (search online, ask neighbors, call experts) to provide the correct answers but he/she must answer question directly. Each question must be answered in 1 minute or less. Staff are free to support other group volunteers if the volunteers in your group lost and are out from game.

The volunteer who provides the most correct answers and stay to end of the game will win and get an award. All staff in the winner's group will come to stage to get a group picture.
